Lasagne

About This Recipe

This is Mary Berry's signature Lasagne that she has been perfecting over the course of her storied career.

This Lasagne tastes best if left to rest for six hours before cooking.

Ingredients

For the Ragu

  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 900g Beef Mince
  • 2 Onions, roughly chopped
  • 4 sticks of Celery, diced
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, crushed
  • 2 level tbsp Plain Flour
  • 150ml Beef Stock
  • 1 tbsp Redcurrant Jelly
  • 3 tbsp Tomato Puree
  • 1 tbsp Chopped Thyme
  • 2x 400g cans Chopped tomatoes

For the White Sauce

  • 50g Butter
  • 50g Plain Flour
  • 750ml Hot Milk
  • 2 tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 50g Parmesan, finely grated
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground Black Pepper

For the Lasagne

  • 12 Lasagne Sheets
  • 75g Mature Cheddar, grated
